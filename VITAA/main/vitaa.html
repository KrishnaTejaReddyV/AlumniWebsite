Chapter Development & Activity Cell

Over the years VITAA has spread its network over various cities in India and across the globe. The first chapter was inaugurated at Chennai during the year 1997. This was followed by other chapters at Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chittoor, Calcutta and Delhi. International Chapters were also developed and inaugurated by our beloved Chancellor during his visits to various countries like Singapore, Australia, Germany, USA & Rwanda.

VIT Corpus Fund - an invitation to make a meaningful difference
